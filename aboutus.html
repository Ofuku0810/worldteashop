About Us

The World Tea Shop is a tea shop that offers high-quality teas from Myanmar, Japan, and Vietnam. We also have seasonal teas that are perfect for any occasion. Our teas are carefully selected to ensure that our customers get the best quality at a reasonable price.

We believe that tea time should be an enjoyable experience, and we strive to make that possible for our customers. Our teas are perfect for any occasion, whether you're looking for a relaxing afternoon tea or a refreshing iced tea on a hot summer day.

At The World Tea Shop, we are committed to providing our customers with the best possible experience. We believe that our teas are the best in the world, and we want to share that with you. Come visit us today and experience the world of tea!
