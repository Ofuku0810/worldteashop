Burmese Classic tea

A traditional beverage from Myanmar, is made with strong black tea and a blend of evaporated and condensed milk. The tea is simmered with a hint of salt, creating a robust base. 'Pulled' like teh tarik, it forms a frothy layer. Enjoyed at tea shops with Burmese fritters and sweets.
